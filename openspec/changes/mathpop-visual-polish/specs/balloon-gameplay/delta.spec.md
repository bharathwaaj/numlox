## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Horizontal Wobble Animation
The system SHALL apply a gentle horizontal oscillation (wobble) to balloons as they rise.

#### Scenario: Balloon movement
- **WHEN** a balloon is moving upward
- **THEN** it SHALL also move horizontally in a periodic (sine-wave-like) manner
- **AND** the horizontal movement SHALL be subtle enough not to interfere with gameplay/tapping
